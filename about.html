About Nails by Riz

Your Premier Destination for Nail Art Excellence

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Nails by Riz began as a small dream and has blossomed into a premier nail salon. Our journey is built on passion, dedication, and the pursuit of nail artistry excellence.

Our Mission

We are committed to providing exceptional nail care services that combine creativity with precision. Our goal is to make every client feel beautiful, confident, and thoroughly pampered.

Our Values

Quality, hygiene, and customer satisfaction are at the heart of everything we do. We believe in using premium products and maintaining the highest standards of cleanliness and service.

Meet Our Team

Reesa Ann Macaraeg

Founder & Lead Nail Artist

With over 5 years of experience in nail artistry, Reesa brings creativity and precision to every service. Specializing in both classic and innovative nail designs, she is dedicated to providing exceptional nail care experiences that leave clients feeling confident and beautiful.
